PUT EMORY'S PHOTOS IN THIS FOLDER
=================================

The website looks for two photos by these exact file names. Save your
pictures here and rename them exactly like this (all lowercase):

1) hero.jpg    -> The big round photo at the very top of the site.
                  Best pick: the close-up of Emory with the peach flower
                  headband looking at the camera, or the clean white-background
                  studio photo. Any nice square-ish photo works.

2) birth.jpg   -> The photo next to her birth details ("I'm Emory").
                  Best pick: the sleeping newborn photo with the wooden
                  "Emory Solberg" name sign.

Notes:
- JPG or PNG both work, but keep the name ending in .jpg
  (or change the name in index.html to match your file).
- If a photo is missing, the site still looks great, it just shows a soft
  pink circle instead. So you can add them anytime.
- Want more photos in a little gallery? Tell Claude and it can add a
  gallery section using the rest of your pictures.
